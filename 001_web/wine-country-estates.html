Temecula Wine Country at a Glance

Temecula Wine Country is one of Southern California's most prestigious residential enclaves. Situated along Rancho California Road and De Portola Road, this area combines rural tranquility with world-class amenities, all just 60 miles north of San Diego and 90 miles southeast of Los Angeles.

Temecula Wine Country is a designated American Viticultural Area (AVA) known for its Mediterranean climate, rolling hills, and stunning sunsets. The region's micro-climate - warm days and cool nights created by the Rainbow Gap - produces exceptional wine grapes and creates an ideal living environment year-round. Properties here are distinguished by their generous acreage, panoramic views of vineyards and mountains, and a pace of life that feels worlds away from the Southern California hustle, while still being conveniently close to urban amenities, excellent schools, and major transportation corridors.

Property Types in Wine Country

Wine Country offers a diverse range of luxury property types, each with its own unique appeal and lifestyle advantages.

Estates on Acreage

The signature Wine Country property: custom-built homes on 1 to 20+ acre parcels with sweeping views of vineyards, mountains, and valleys. These estates typically feature 3,000 to 6,000+ square feet of living space, resort-style pools, outdoor kitchens, and detached guest houses or casitas. Many include RV garages, workshops, and extensive hardscaping. Prices range from $900K for smaller parcels to well over $3 million for premier estates.

Horse Properties

Temecula Wine Country is an equestrian paradise with numerous properties featuring horse facilities including stables, arenas, round pens, turnouts, and direct access to riding trails. The region's mild climate allows year-round riding. Properties with horse amenities typically sit on 2 to 10+ acres and range from $850K to $2.5 million depending on the home, land, and equestrian improvements.

Vineyard Homes

For those who dream of owning their own vineyard, Temecula offers properties with existing planted vines or land suitable for viticulture. Some owners produce wine commercially through local wineries, while others maintain vineyards purely for the lifestyle and aesthetic. These properties are among the most unique and sought-after in the region, often commanding premium prices for established vine acreage.

Custom Builds

Vacant land parcels in Wine Country present the opportunity to build your dream home from the ground up. Parcels range from 1 to 40+ acres with various terrain, views, and zoning options. George can connect you with trusted local builders, architects, and land specialists to navigate the permitting process and bring your vision to life. Land prices vary widely based on location, views, and utilities availability.

World-Class Amenities at Your Doorstep

Wine Country living offers more than stunning properties. The area is surrounded by exceptional dining, entertainment, and lifestyle amenities that rival any luxury destination.

Award-Winning Wineries

Over 50 wineries line Rancho California Road and De Portola Road, offering daily tastings, live music, gourmet dining, and stunning event venues. Popular destinations include Wilson Creek, Ponte Winery, South Coast Winery, Leoness Cellars, and Callaway Vineyard. Many Wine Country residents enjoy walking or biking to their favorite tasting room for a weekend afternoon.

Fine Dining & Restaurants

From winery restaurants like The Restaurant at Ponte and Meritage at Callaway to Old Town Temecula's eclectic dining scene, the area offers exceptional culinary experiences. Farm-to-table restaurants, craft breweries, and international cuisines provide endless options. Old Town's Front Street features dozens of restaurants, cafes, and bars within a walkable district.

Old Town & Recreation

Historic Old Town Temecula offers antique shopping, art galleries, live theater at the Old Town Temecula Community Theater, and a vibrant nightlife. Outdoor recreation includes hot air balloon rides over the vineyards, hiking in the Santa Rosa Plateau Ecological Reserve, golf at several championship courses, and the Temecula Valley Polo Club. Pechanga Resort Casino is also minutes away.
