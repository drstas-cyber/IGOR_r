Types of Alerts You Can Receive

George can configure multiple alert types to keep you informed about every opportunity in the market. Most clients use a combination of these alerts to stay ahead of the competition.

New Listings

The most popular alert type. Get notified the moment a new home matching your criteria is listed on the MLS. In Temecula Valley's fast-moving market where desirable homes can receive offers within 24-48 hours, these instant alerts give you a critical head start. You will see the listing before it appears on consumer websites, giving you time to schedule a showing and prepare your offer strategy.

Price Reductions

Price reduction alerts notify you when a home in your target area drops its asking price. This is a powerful tool for value-conscious buyers. A price drop can signal a motivated seller, creating an opportunity to negotiate an even better deal. George can set up price reduction alerts for specific neighborhoods or across entire cities to help you spot bargains as they emerge.

Open Houses

Receive weekly summaries of upcoming open houses in your preferred areas. Open houses give you the chance to tour homes in person without scheduling individual appointments, explore neighborhoods you are considering, and get a feel for what is available in your price range. George can also arrange private showings for any home you discover through an open house alert.

Back on Market

Sometimes the best opportunities come from homes that were previously under contract but fell out of escrow. Back-on-market alerts notify you when these properties become available again. These can be excellent opportunities because the seller may be more motivated to close quickly, and there is often less competition since many buyers already dismissed the listing. A back-on-market home could be the hidden gem you have been waiting for.
