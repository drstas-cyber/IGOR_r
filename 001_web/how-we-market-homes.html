How We Market Your Home

Professional HDR Photography

High-dynamic-range photography captures your home's best features with perfect lighting, wide angles, and stunning clarity that makes buyers stop scrolling and start scheduling showings.

Virtual Tours & Video

Immersive 3D virtual tours and cinematic video walkthroughs let out-of-area buyers experience your home remotely, expanding your buyer pool well beyond the local market.

MLS & IDX Distribution

Your listing is syndicated across the MLS and hundreds of real estate websites including Zillow, Realtor.com, Redfin, Trulia, and every major home search platform for maximum reach.

Social Media Marketing

Email Marketing to Buyer Agents

Direct email campaigns to a network of active buyer's agents in Riverside, San Diego, and surrounding counties ensure agents with qualified buyers know about your listing immediately.

Open Houses

Professionally hosted open houses create urgency and competition. George personally greets visitors, highlights your home's best features, and follows up with every interested buyer.

Print Materials

Premium property brochures, flyers, and just-listed postcards distributed throughout the neighborhood and at open houses create a polished, professional impression for your listing.

Targeted Online Ads

Pay-per-click campaigns on Google and Facebook target buyers actively searching for homes in Temecula, Murrieta, and surrounding areas, driving qualified traffic directly to your listing.
