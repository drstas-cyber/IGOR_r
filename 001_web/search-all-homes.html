Browse by City

Temecula Valley spans several thriving cities, each with its own character, amenities, and price points. Explore the community that fits your lifestyle and budget.

Temecula

Median Home Price: ~$740,000

The heart of Temecula Valley offers wine country estates, master-planned communities like Wolf Creek and Redhawk, top-rated schools in the Temecula Valley Unified School District, and vibrant Old Town dining and entertainment. From luxury hilltop homes to affordable starter properties, Temecula has something for every buyer.

Murrieta

Median Home Price: ~$650,000

Consistently ranked among the safest cities in California, Murrieta features family-friendly neighborhoods, excellent schools, and a growing commercial center. Popular communities include Greer Ranch, Spencer's Crossing, and The Oaks. Easy access to I-15 and I-215 makes commuting convenient.

Menifee

Median Home Price: ~$560,000

One of the fastest-growing cities in the Inland Empire, Menifee offers exceptional value with newer construction, master-planned communities, and rapidly expanding retail and dining options. Popular areas include Audie Murphy Ranch and Heritage Lake. Great for first-time buyers and growing families.

Winchester

Median Home Price: ~$620,000

A census-designated place within the Temecula Valley Unified School District, Winchester offers newer homes, larger lots, and a more rural feel while maintaining proximity to Temecula and Murrieta amenities. French Valley Airport and the growing commercial corridor provide added convenience.

Lake Elsinore

Median Home Price: ~$530,000

Centered around its namesake natural lake, Lake Elsinore offers waterfront living, mountain recreation, and some of the most affordable homes in the region. The Storm Stadium, skydiving, and water sports make it a recreational paradise. Canyon Hills and Tuscany Hills are popular neighborhoods.

Wildomar

Median Home Price: ~$580,000

Nestled between Murrieta and Lake Elsinore, Wildomar provides a small-town atmosphere with modern conveniences. The community is known for equestrian properties, larger lots, and a relaxed pace of life. Its central location offers easy access to I-15 for commuters heading north or south.

Fallbrook

Median Home Price: ~$790,000

Known as the "Friendly Village," Fallbrook is famous for its avocado groves, rolling hills, and charming downtown. Located in northern San Diego County, it offers larger ranch-style properties, horse trails, and a strong sense of community. The annual Avocado Festival draws visitors from across the region.

Browse by Property Type

Whether you are looking for your first condo, a spacious family home, a luxury estate, or an active adult community, Temecula Valley has options to fit your lifestyle.

🏠

Single Family Homes

The most popular property type in Temecula Valley, single-family homes range from affordable 3-bedroom starter homes in the $400Ks to spacious 5+ bedroom estates over $1 million. Most neighborhoods feature community pools, parks, and walking trails. Lot sizes typically range from 5,000 to 20,000+ square feet.

🏢

Condos & Townhomes

An excellent entry point into the Temecula Valley market, condos and townhomes offer lower maintenance living with HOA-maintained exteriors, community amenities, and prices starting in the mid-$300Ks. Ideal for first-time buyers, investors, and those who prefer a lock-and-leave lifestyle.

🌟

Luxury Estates

Temecula Valley's luxury market includes Wine Country estates on multi-acre parcels, hilltop homes with panoramic views in Morgan Hill and Crown Hill, custom-built properties with resort-style amenities, and gated communities offering privacy and exclusivity. Prices range from $800K to well over $2 million.

☀️

55+ Communities

Active adult communities in Temecula Valley offer resort-style living for those 55 and older. Enjoy clubhouses, fitness centers, pools, golf courses, and organized social activities. Communities like Solera Diamond Valley and Four Seasons at Murrieta provide maintenance-free living in a vibrant, social setting.
