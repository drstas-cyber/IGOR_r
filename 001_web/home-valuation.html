Why a Professional CMA Beats Automated Estimates

Online home value tools from Zillow, Redfin, and Realtor.com use algorithms that often miss critical details. Here's why George's professional analysis delivers a more accurate picture.

Automated Estimates (Zillow, Redfin)

  • Use publicly available data that is often outdated or incomplete
  • Cannot see upgrades, renovations, or condition of your home
  • Miss neighborhood-specific micro-trends and nuances
  • Median error rate of 6-8%, which on a $740K home is $44K-$59K off
  • Don't account for lot size differences, views, or location within community

George's Professional CMA

  • Analyzes true comparable sales with adjustments for differences
  • Considers your home's specific upgrades, condition, and unique features
  • 14+ years of neighborhood-level Temecula Valley expertise
  • Accounts for current buyer demand, seasonal trends, and market shifts
  • Provides a strategic pricing recommendation to maximize your sale price
