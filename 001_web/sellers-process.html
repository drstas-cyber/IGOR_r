Your 8-Step Selling Journey

Here is exactly what to expect when you list your Temecula Valley home with George Khazanovskiy.

1

Initial Consultation & CMA

George meets with you to understand your goals, timeline, and expectations. He prepares a detailed Comparative Market Analysis (CMA) to determine the optimal listing price based on recent comparable sales in your neighborhood. This meeting is completely free and comes with no obligation.

2

Prep Your Home

George provides a personalized checklist of improvements and staging recommendations to maximize your home's appeal. This might include decluttering, minor repairs, fresh paint, or landscaping touch-ups. Every suggestion is tailored to deliver the highest return on your investment of time and money.

3

Professional Photography & Marketing Materials

A professional photographer captures HDR photos, virtual tours, and video content that showcase your home at its absolute best. George oversees the creation of all marketing materials including property brochures, online listings, and social media content designed to generate maximum buyer interest.

4

List on MLS & Launch Marketing

Your home goes live on the MLS and is syndicated across hundreds of real estate websites including Zillow, Realtor.com, Redfin, and Trulia. Simultaneously, George launches targeted social media campaigns, email blasts to buyer agents, and online advertising to create an immediate wave of interest.

5

Showings & Open Houses

George coordinates and manages all showings, providing feedback from every visit. Professional open houses are hosted to generate foot traffic and create buyer urgency. You receive regular updates on showing activity, buyer interest levels, and market feedback so you always know where things stand.

6

Receive & Negotiate Offers

When offers come in, George carefully reviews each one and presents them to you with his professional analysis. He negotiates on your behalf to secure the strongest possible terms, including price, contingencies, closing timeline, and any repairs. His goal is to protect your interests while keeping the deal on track.

7

Inspections, Appraisal & Escrow

Once you accept an offer, George manages the escrow process, coordinating home inspections, the appraisal, and all required paperwork. He communicates with the buyer's agent, lender, title company, and escrow officer to ensure everything stays on schedule and any issues are resolved quickly.

8

Close & Celebrate

On closing day, you sign the final documents, hand over the keys, and receive your proceeds. George ensures a smooth closing by reviewing all documents beforehand and being available to address any last-minute questions. Congratulations — your home is sold, and it is time to celebrate your next chapter!
